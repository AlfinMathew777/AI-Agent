# SOP – Front Office: Check-in & Check-out
Southern Horizons Hotel – Hobart, Tasmania  
Version: 1.0 | Last updated: Nov 2025

## 1. Purpose
To provide a consistent, warm and efficient check-in and check-out experience for all guests.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all Front Office and Reception team members.

---

## 3. Standard Check-in Procedure

**Standard check-in time:** 2:00 pm

When a guest arrives at the front desk:

1. **Greet the guest**
   - Smile and make eye contact.
   - Use the greeting:  
     > “Good morning / afternoon / evening, welcome to Southern Horizons Hotel. How may I help you today?”

2. **Confirm reservation**
   - Ask: “May I please have your surname and a photo ID?”
   - Search by surname in the PMS (Property Management System).
   - Confirm:
     - Number of nights
     - Room type and bed configuration
     - Number of guests
     - Rate type (e.g. corporate, package, OTA booking)

3. **Verify payment method**
   - Pre-authorise credit card or take payment according to booking type.
   - Explain any hold or security deposit clearly.
   - For walk-in guests, follow “Walk-in Booking” process (separate SOP if needed).

4. **Issue room keys**
   - Encode key cards and confirm room number quietly (do not shout it).
   - Example script:  
     > “You are in room 612 on level 6. Lifts are just around the corner.”

5. **Provide essential information**
   - Check-out time (10:00 am).
   - Breakfast times and location.
   - Wi-Fi details (network name and password).
   - Parking information if applicable.
   - Restaurant/bar opening hours.

6. **Offer assistance**
   - “Would you like any help with your luggage or directions to your room?”

7. **Close the interaction**
   - “We hope you enjoy your stay. Please dial 9 from your room phone if you need anything.”

---

## 4. Early Check-in Requests

1. Check room status in PMS and with Housekeeping.
2. If a clean room is available:
   - Offer immediate check-in.
3. If no room is ready:
   - Offer to store luggage.
   - Offer use of lobby, Wi-Fi and facilities where appropriate.
4. Never promise early check-in until room status is confirmed.

---

## 5. Check-out Procedure

**Standard check-out time:** 10:00 am

1. Greet the guest:
   > “Good morning, how was your stay with us?”

2. Confirm identity:
   - Ask for room number and surname.
   - Confirm details in PMS.

3. Review charges:
   - Room charges, restaurant, minibar, parking, etc.
   - Check if there are disputes and handle politely.

4. Process payment:
   - Use existing pre-authorisation where possible.
   - Offer tax invoice if required.

5. Ask for feedback:
   - “Is there anything we could have done to make your stay better?”
   - Log any serious feedback or complaints in the system.

6. Farewell:
   - “Thank you for staying at Southern Horizons Hotel. We hope to welcome you again soon.”

---

## 6. Late Check-out

1. Check occupancy and housekeeping schedule.
2. Approved late check-out examples:
   - 11:00 am – usually complimentary (case by case).
   - 12:00 pm or later – may incur a fee.
3. Clearly communicate any fee and update PMS.
4. Inform Housekeeping of the revised departure time.

---

## 7. No-show & Cancellation (Summary)

- Follow the booking policy for:
  - Guaranteed bookings
  - No-shows
  - Late cancellations
- Ensure charges match the rate policy or corporate agreement.

# Guest Facilities & Services
Southern Horizons Hotel – Hobart, Tasmania  
Version: 1.1 | Last updated: Nov 2025

## Housekeeping

- Daily housekeeping service is provided.
- For extra towels, pillows or amenities, contact Reception.
- Use the “Do Not Disturb” sign if you do not wish to be serviced.

---

## Laundry

- Guest laundry or laundry service is available – please check with Reception for:
  - Location of guest laundry (if provided), or
  - Laundry bags and price list for paid service.

---

## Fitness & Relaxation

Facilities may vary by season and package, but can include:

- Fitness room or small gym for light exercise.
- Guest lounge area for reading or quiet work.
- Outdoor seating areas or courtyard (weather permitting).

Please check Reception or lobby signage for opening hours and access instructions.

---

## “What can I do in the hotel to enjoy my day?”

Depending on your interests and the facilities available, you might:

- Enjoy a relaxed breakfast or coffee in the restaurant.
- Spend time reading or working in the lounge.
- Use the gym or go for a walk along the waterfront nearby.
- Book a later checkout (subject to availability and fees) to enjoy a slow morning.
- Ask Reception for board games, local walking maps or rainy-day suggestions.

---

## Business Services

- Basic printing or scanning may be available at Reception (fees may apply).
- Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel for email and online meetings.

---

## Transport & Transfers

- Taxi and rideshare services (e.g. Uber) operate in Hobart.
- Airport transfers or shuttle bookings can be arranged via Reception with notice.

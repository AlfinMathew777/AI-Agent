# Guest Compendium – General Information
Southern Horizons Hotel – Hobart, Tasmania  
Version: 1.1 | Last updated: Nov 2025

## Welcome

Welcome to Southern Horizons Hotel in Hobart. Our goal is to make your stay comfortable, relaxed and memorable. If you are ever unsure about anything in your room or the hotel, please contact Reception – we are happy to help.

---

## Check-in & Check-out

- **Check-in time:** 2:00 pm  
- **Check-out time:** 10:00 am  

Late check-out may be available (fees may apply). Please contact Reception the day before departure.

---

## Reception

- Reception is staffed 24 hours a day.
- Dial **9** from your room phone for assistance at any time.

---

## Wi-Fi

- Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
- Network name and password are provided on your key wallet or in-room welcome card.
- If you have trouble connecting, please:
  1. Check that Wi-Fi is enabled on your device.
  2. Select the correct network.
  3. Enter the password exactly as shown (case-sensitive).
  4. If it still doesn’t work, contact Reception and we will assist.

---

## Parking

- Limited on-site parking is available.
- Charges may apply depending on your booking.
- Please ask Reception about availability, height limits and current prices.

---

## Smoking Policy

- Our hotel is **100% smoke-free** inside all rooms and indoor public areas.
- Designated smoking areas are located outside the building.
- A cleaning fee may be charged if smoking occurs inside rooms.

---

## Noise & Consideration

Please be considerate of other guests by keeping noise to a minimum, especially between **10:00 pm and 7:00 am**. If you are disturbed by noise, contact Reception so we can assist.

---

## “Is this a good hotel?”

We aim to provide:

- Clean, comfortable rooms.
- Friendly, Tasmanian-style hospitality.
- Food and drinks that showcase local produce.
- Helpful local recommendations so you can make the most of your time in Hobart.

If anything is not right, please tell us during your stay so we can fix it rather than after you leave.

# Guest Dining – Breakfast, Restaurant & Room Service
Southern Horizons Hotel – Hobart, Tasmania  
Version: 1.1 | Last updated: Nov 2025

## Breakfast

- **Weekdays:** 6:30 am – 10:00 am  
- **Weekends & Public Holidays:** 7:00 am – 10:30 am  
- Location: Ground floor restaurant (signs near lifts).

Breakfast typically includes:

- Cooked breakfast items (eggs, bacon, grilled tomato, mushrooms).
- Cereals, yoghurt and fresh fruit.
- Pastries and toast.
- Tea, coffee and juices.

If breakfast is included in your rate, this will be shown on your booking. If you are unsure, ask Reception.

---

## “Is the food good here? What do you recommend?”

Our kitchen focuses on **simple, good-quality dishes** using Tasmanian produce where possible. Guests often comment that:

- The breakfast is generous and fresh.
- Hot dishes are cooked to order.
- Coffee is strong and consistent.

Popular items include (subject to menu changes):

- Tasmanian smoked salmon with poached eggs.
- Slow-cooked lamb shoulder or local fish for dinner.
- Local cheese platter featuring Tasmanian cheeses.

If you’re unsure what to order, ask the team for today’s recommendation or chef’s special.

---

## Restaurant & Bar

- Lunch and dinner opening hours are displayed at Reception and in the lobby.
- A selection of Tasmanian wines, beers and ciders is available at the bar.
- We recommend making a booking during busy periods or if you are a larger group.

---

## Room Service

- Room service is available during restaurant opening hours (menu may be limited late at night).
- To order:
  - Dial **In-Room Dining** from your room phone, or  
  - Use any digital ordering system if provided in your room.
- A tray or delivery charge may apply (see menu for details).

---

## Dietary Requirements

Please let us know if you have any:

- Allergies (e.g. nuts, shellfish, dairy).
- Dietary preferences (e.g. vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free).

We will do our best to provide suitable options or adjust dishes where possible.

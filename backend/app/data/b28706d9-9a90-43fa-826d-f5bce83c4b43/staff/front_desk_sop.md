# Southern Horizons Hotel - Standard Operating Procedures

## Front Desk Operations

### Check-In Procedure
1. Greet the guest warmly with a smile
2. Request ID and reservation confirmation
3. Verify booking details in the system
4. Process payment or validate pre-payment
5. Assign room and create key cards
6. Explain hotel amenities and services
7. Offer assistance with luggage
8. Provide WiFi password and breakfast information

### Check-Out Procedure
1. Ask about the guest's stay experience
2. Review any additional charges (minibar, room service)
3. Process final payment
4. Provide receipt/invoice
5. Collect room keys
6. Offer assistance with luggage and transportation
7. Thank guest and invite them to return

### Late Check-Out Policy
- Standard check-out time: 11:00 AM
- Late check-out until 2:00 PM: $25 fee
- Late check-out until 4:00 PM: $50 fee (subject to availability)
- After 4:00 PM: Full additional night charge
- VIP and loyalty members: Complimentary late check-out until 2:00 PM

### Early Check-In Policy
- Standard check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Early check-in from 12:00 PM: Subject to availability, $25 fee
- Guaranteed early check-in: $50 fee (must be requested 24 hours in advance)
- VIP members: Complimentary early check-in when available

### Room Upgrade Procedures
- Upgrades may be offered when:
  - Guest is a loyalty member
  - It's a special occasion (birthday, anniversary)
  - Compensation for service issues
  - Overbooked category
- Always document upgrades in the system
- Inform the guest of the upgrade value
- Manager approval required for complimentary suite upgrades

### Lost Room Key Procedure
1. Verify guest identity (name, room number, ID)
2. Deactivate old key in the system
3. Issue new key card
4. Document incident in guest notes
5. If guest cannot prove identity, escort to room with security

## Guest Complaint Handling

### Standard Complaint Resolution Steps
1. Listen actively without interrupting
2. Apologize sincerely for the inconvenience
3. Take ownership of the problem
4. Offer immediate solution or alternatives
5. Follow up to ensure satisfaction
6. Document in the guest profile

### Compensation Guidelines
- Minor inconvenience: Apology + small gesture (drink voucher)
- Moderate issue: 10-20% room discount or complimentary service
- Serious complaint: Up to 50% room discount or free night
- Major failure: Full refund consideration, manager approval required

### Escalation Procedure
- Level 1: Front desk staff handles
- Level 2: Front desk supervisor (if staff cannot resolve)
- Level 3: Duty manager (for serious complaints)
- Level 4: General Manager (for critical issues or legal concerns)

## Payment Policies

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Discover)
- Cash (foreign currency at reception desk rates)
- Bank transfer (corporate accounts only)
- Mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

### Deposit and Pre-Authorization
- Standard deposit: First night + $50 incidentals
- Extended stays (5+ nights): 50% total + $100 incidentals
- No credit card: Full payment in advance required

### Refund Policy
- Cancellation 72+ hours before: Full refund
- Cancellation 24-72 hours: First night charge
- Cancellation within 24 hours: No refund
- No-show: Full stay charged
- Early departure: No refund for remaining nights

## Emergency Procedures

### Fire Emergency
1. Pull fire alarm if not already activated
2. Call 911 and hotel security
3. Announce evacuation via PA system
4. Guide guests to nearest emergency exits
5. Do NOT use elevators
6. Meet at designated assembly point
7. Account for all guests and staff

### Medical Emergency
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not move the person unless in danger
3. Notify hotel security
4. Clear area of bystanders
5. Provide first aid if trained
6. Document incident thoroughly

### Security Incidents
- Suspicious persons: Report to security immediately
- Theft reports: Document and contact police
- Aggressive guests: Call security, do not confront
- Bomb threats: Follow evacuation protocol, call police

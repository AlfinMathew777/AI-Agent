# Housekeeping Department - Standard Operating Procedures

## Room Cleaning Standards

### Daily Room Cleaning Checklist
1. **Knock and Announce**: Knock three times, announce "Housekeeping"
2. **Wait 10 seconds**, then use key card if no response
3. **Open drapes/curtains** for natural light
4. **Strip bed linens** - place in laundry bag
5. **Clear trash** from all receptacles
6. **Clean bathroom**:
   - Scrub toilet, sink, and tub/shower
   - Replace towels and toiletries
   - Clean mirror and chrome fixtures
   - Mop floor
7. **Make bed** with fresh linens
8. **Dust all surfaces** including furniture, TV, and fixtures
9. **Vacuum carpet** or mop hard floors
10. **Check minibar** and restock if needed
11. **Inspect room** for damage or maintenance issues
12. **Close drapes** to appropriate position
13. **Final walk-through** before exiting

### Linen Change Schedule
- **Sheets**: Daily during stay
- **Duvet covers**: Every 3 days or upon request
- **Bath towels**: Daily replacement
- **Hand towels**: Daily replacement
- **Bathmat**: Every 2 days
- **Pillowcases**: Daily

### Room Turnover Timing
- Standard room: 25-30 minutes
- Deluxe room: 35-40 minutes
- Suite: 45-60 minutes
- Penthouse: 60-90 minutes

## Maintenance Reporting

### Issue Identification
- Check for water damage, stains, and mold
- Test all light fixtures and bulbs
- Verify HVAC operation
- Inspect furniture for damage
- Check TV and remote function
- Test door locks and safety latches

### Reporting Procedure
1. Document issue in the maintenance log
2. Report to housekeeping supervisor immediately
3. Mark room as "Out of Service" if necessary
4. Notify front desk of room status change
5. Follow up on repair completion

### Priority Levels
- **Urgent**: Safety hazards, flooding, no hot water - report immediately
- **High**: AC/heating failure, plumbing issues - same day
- **Medium**: Cosmetic damage, minor repairs - within 24 hours
- **Low**: Preventive maintenance - scheduled

## Lost and Found Procedure

### Item Discovery
1. Do NOT touch suspicious or valuable items
2. Call supervisor for high-value items (jewelry, electronics, cash)
3. Document item description, location, date, and time
4. Place in sealed bag with documentation
5. Deliver to Lost and Found storage

### Item Retention
- Perishable items: 24 hours
- Clothing and personal items: 90 days
- Valuables: 6 months
- ID documents: Contact guest immediately

### Return Process
1. Verify guest identity and item description
2. Record return in log
3. Guest signs receipt
4. Ship items at guest's expense if requested

## Safety Procedures

### Chemical Handling
- Always read product labels before use
- Wear appropriate PPE (gloves, eye protection)
- Never mix cleaning chemicals
- Store chemicals in designated areas
- Report spills immediately

### Bloodborne Pathogen Protocol
1. Do NOT touch blood or bodily fluids
2. Call supervisor immediately
3. Secure the area
4. Use biohazard kit for cleanup (trained personnel only)
5. Dispose of contaminated materials properly
6. Document incident

### Needle/Sharp Object Protocol
1. Do NOT attempt to pick up
2. Call supervisor immediately
3. Use sharps container for disposal
4. Report to security if drug paraphernalia suspected

## Deep Cleaning Procedures

### Quarterly Deep Clean Checklist
- Steam clean all carpets
- Wash all curtains/drapes
- Clean behind and under furniture
- Sanitize mattress
- Shampoo upholstered furniture
- Clean air vents and filters
- Descale bathroom fixtures
- Polish all wood surfaces

### Turndown Service Standards
1. Scheduled for 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
2. Remove bedspread, turn down sheets at 45-degree angle
3. Close drapes
4. Turn on bedside lamp
5. Place chocolates/mints on pillow
6. Replenish towels if needed
7. Empty trash
8. Adjust room temperature

## Supply Management

### Par Stock Levels (Per Floor)
- Sheets: 20 sets
- Towels (bath): 40 pieces
- Towels (hand): 40 pieces
- Toiletries kits: 30 sets
- Cleaning supplies: 1 week supply

### Ordering Procedure
1. Check inventory daily
2. Submit requisition when at 50% par
3. Supervisor approval required
4. Emergency orders: Contact housekeeping manager
